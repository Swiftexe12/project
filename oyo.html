About This Event

The Oyo Agribusiness Summit is project-managed by Agro4you Limited in conjunction with the Oyo State Agribusiness Development Agency (OYSADA), in line with Governor Seyi Makinde's vision and road-map towards changing the agricultural landscape of the state to become the agribusiness hub of Nigeria.

Oyo State Facts

Oyo State covers an area of 28,454 square kilometers, with about 2.5 million hectares of fertile agricultural land available for commercial cultivation. At the Oyo State Agribusiness Summit, you will be informed about the tremendous business opportunities that exist in the Oyo State's Agricultural sector, which constitutes a huge percentage of the economy of the state.

A Message from the Governor

Oyo State's agribusiness sector was making remarkable strides in 2020 in line with our vision to become a leading global producer of agricultural products that meet international standards and Africa number one hub for processing, packaging, and marketing of agricultural products. Then Covid-19 hit and sparked unprecedented disruption. The pandemic brought the global agri-food industry under great distress. However, here in Oyo State, we responded in a timely manner and with great resilience to inoculate our food system and ensure supply lines were kept open. The pandemic has ensured Nigeria's economy is facing severe challenges and no one doubts the key role agribusiness will play in our recovery. Agriculture and agribusiness hold the key for the accelerated growth, diversification, and job creation for Oyo State in particular.
